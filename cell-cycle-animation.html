Cell Cycle Animation

INTERPHASE PROPHASE METAPHASE ANAPHASE TELOPHASE / CYTOKINESIS
Interphase: Cell grows, DNA replicates.
Prophase: Chromosomes condense, nuclear membrane dissolves.
Metaphase: Chromosomes align at equator.
Anaphase: Sister chromatids separate, move to poles.
Telophase/Cytokinesis: Nuclei reform, cell divides into two.
